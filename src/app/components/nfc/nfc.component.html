NFC Tap for Payments:
Storm SDK enables users with NFC-enabled devices to initiate quick and secure payments by simply tapping on a POS terminal. The integration of the storm library ensures that transactions are processed efficiently and securely through the STORM network, providing a seamless payment experience for customers.
